
# AI-Powered Lead Generation Engine
Automated lead sourcing, qualification, and outreach system for B2B companies

# Company Background
ScaleSprint Marketing is a growth agency serving SaaS and ecommerce businesses. They needed a scalable lead generation system that could replace expensive paid advertising while reducing manual prospecting work.

# Problem Statement
Challenge: Manual lead generation was expensive, time-consuming, and inconsistent.

High cost per lead from paid ads ($150-200 per qualified lead)
Sales team spending 20+ hours weekly on research and prospecting
Low reply rates (4-6%) from generic outreach templates
No systematic way to identify and prioritize high-value prospects
Limited scalability without hiring more SDRs

Core issue: Growth was bottlenecked by manual processes that couldn't scale profitably.

# Architecture Design
A five-stage automated pipeline that operates continuously:
# 1. Lead Sourcing
Automatically searches Google Maps and Apollo.io daily for businesses matching ideal customer profile (industry, size, location).
# 2. Data Enrichment
Finds decision-maker emails and validates deliverability to ensure high inbox placement.
# 3. AI Qualification
Claude AI visits each company's website, analyzes their marketing stack and funnel, identifies pain points, and scores leads 0-100. Only top-scoring leads proceed.
# 4. Personalized Outreach
AI generates custom email copy based on specific business insights—no generic templates. Automated sending and follow-up sequences.
# 5. CRM Tracking
Centralized dashboard tracks all leads, qualification scores, email status, and responses in real-time.

# Tools Used
Google Maps API - Local business discovery
Apollo.io - B2B company database
Hunter.io - Email finding
NeverBounce - Email validation
Claude AI - Lead qualification and email writing
n8n - Workflow automation
Google Sheets - CRM and reporting
Gmail API - Email delivery
